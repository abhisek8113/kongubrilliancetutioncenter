About Us

Kongu Brilliance Tuition Centre is a premier educational institution based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Founded with a mission to provide quality education at an affordable cost, we specialize in personalized coaching for students from Class 1 to Class 12 across all major boards — CBSE, ICSE, ISC, IGCSE and Tamil Nadu State Board.

Our centre is located at 1/35A Gandhi Street, Vadamalaipalayam, Behind Ramakrishna Engineering College, Coimbatore - 641022. We also provide home tuition across all major areas of Coimbatore including RS Puram, Gandhipuram, Saibaba Colony, Singanallur, Vadavalli, Podanur, Peelamedu, Hope College area and beyond.

At Kongu Brilliance, Centum is our standard. We believe every child has the potential to excel, and our role is to unlock that potential through the right guidance, discipline, and support.

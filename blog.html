Quick Study Tips from Kongu Brilliance Tutors

Read Full Article →

Review Every Day

Spend 15 minutes each evening reviewing what you learned that day. Regular revision beats cramming before exams.

Practice Past Papers

Solve last 5 years board exam papers. You will notice patterns and be prepared for any question format.

Sleep Well

A well-rested brain learns and retains information better. Aim for 8 hours of sleep especially before exams.

Ask Questions

Never hesitate to ask your tutor when you don't understand. One unresolved doubt can block understanding of the whole topic.

Create Mind Maps

Visual summaries of topics help you remember concepts during exams. Draw mind maps for history, science, and geography.

Study Groups

Explaining concepts to peers helps reinforce your own understanding. Form small study groups for discussion sessions.
